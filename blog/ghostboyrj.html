Artist Feature: Ghostboyrj

April 23, 2026

Ghostboyrj (Rohan White) is a Toronto-born, Brampton-raised hip-hop producer and artist building a steadily expanding catalog in the city's underground.

In 2026, he's been attached to notable releases for other artists, including Eyeda Sophia's (In)Sense and Crooks' The Principle of Least Interest (TPOLI), the latter co-produced with Celinee, Jake ETC, and HEARTBEATZ. Both projects share a common throughline — sharp lyricism anchored by White's textured, sample-forward production.

On his own front, White released Cartoons & Cereal (2026), a 12-track LP executive-produced by Jake ETC and released via Ghostown Records / URBNET. It positions him as a serious album-maker, not just a beat supplier.

Before all of this came a steady run: ghoststories and ghoststories (Deluxe) earlier in 2026, and a catalog stretching back through ghostboy, ghostboy2, State of Mind, and Peak Performance — each release refining his production voice.

Ghostboyrj's sound leans heavy on 808s, sample-driven loops, and a beat-first mentality. It's production that speaks through texture and atmosphere, with a lo-fi edge that's matured into sharper, more deliberate arrangements.

What makes Ghostboyrj worth paying attention to isn't a single viral moment. It's consistency and community: he's producing for other artists, curating compilations, and releasing his own work — all while building infrastructure for the Toronto scene around him.

Artist Feature: livingthing

April 23, 2026

livingthing is a Toronto-based indie electronic artist commanding attention on Spotify's Fresh Finds Canada, where his dreamy synth-pop has driven 348K monthly listeners.

His sound carves a distinct lane: glitchy electronic beats layered with ethereal vocals and introspective lyrics, blending vulnerability with dancefloor-ready energy. It's production that nods to early Grimes or Tinashe while rooting firmly in Toronto's club underground—perfect for late-night drives or festival openers.

The breakout single "auburn" helped establish livingthing's profile, while the 2026 EP juniper ring gathers the earlier singles "orchid," "girl don't cry," and "skincontact" alongside two new tracks, making it a fuller and more focused release.

What makes livingthing 2026's breakout indie electronic act isn't viral hype alone—it's the steady listener climb and back-to-back EPs signaling festival slots ahead. He's the electronic pulse Toronto needs right now.

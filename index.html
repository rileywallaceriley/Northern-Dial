About

Northern Dial is an independent Canadian radio station built for people who actually care about music.

This station exists because Canadian music deserves space not just in quotas, but in context. We believe the best songs hit differently depending on the hour, the season, and where you are. So we built a station that moves with you.

We stream 24/7 and add new music into rotation constantly. Interested in having your music or podcast played? Scroll down and click our submission box.

The Northern Dial Project

Our Mantra

Northern Dial exists to prove that independent, community-driven radio can thrive in the streaming era. We believe Canadian music deserves better than algorithm playlists and quota-filling: it deserves context, curation, and a platform built by people who actually care.

Where We Are Now

This is v1: a homegrown, open-source passion project built from scratch. We're streaming 24/7 with curated blocks of Canadian music across genres, constantly adding new tracks to rotation, and developing the infrastructure for what comes next.

The Vision

Northern Dial is positioning to become a comprehensive Canadian music and culture platform with:

  • Hyper-local niche programming: city-specific shows, regional spotlights, and genre-focused blocks
  • Weekly shows and segments: hosted programs with personalities, guest mixes, artist interviews
  • Community news integration: local music scene coverage, event promotion, grassroots storytelling
  • Personality development: cultivating hosts and voices that connect with listeners
  • Exclusive and live content: live DJ sets, in-studio sessions, premiere releases
  • Complementary media website: reviews, features, news, and longform content supporting what's on air

This isn't just a radio station. It's a movement to reclaim space for independent Canadian voices in a landscape dominated by corporate consolidation and algorithmic curation.

How You Can Contribute

Northern Dial is community-powered. We welcome contributions in any capacity:

  • Sponsorship and Funding: Help us expand infrastructure, commission content, and compensate contributors
  • Grassroots Promotion: Share the station, spread the word, build the community
  • Content Submission: Submit music, podcasts, mixes, or ideas for programming
  • Development and Design: Contribute code, design assets, or technical expertise
  • Hosting and Production: Pitch show concepts, volunteer to host, produce segments
  • Writing and Journalism: Contribute to the future media platform with reviews, features, and reporting
